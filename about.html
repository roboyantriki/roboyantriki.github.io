The Robotics club of SNU is formed to enlighten the spirit of robotics and technology among students. The robotics club aims at honing the skills of the students in terms of technology and its applications. At the robotics club the students can not only enhance their knowledge of robotics and how they perceive technology but also add to the clubs existing knowledge base. There’s scope for both creativity and innovation when comes to robotics. The club is looking forward to host a plethora of events in the coming academic year and there’s a lot in store. The club is a dynamic platform for technology enthusiasts. Watch this space for more.
